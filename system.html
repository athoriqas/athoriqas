02. Why Do You Love Me Leads TJ Ruth to Win the 2023 FFWI Award, Becoming the First in Acting

His acting as Endang in the film Why Do You Love Me made the jury choose him as the winner.

TJ Ruth managed to beat Adinia Wirasti, Maya Hasan, Tissa Biani and Asri Welas in this category.

Unable to contain his emotion, TJ Ruth cried when he received the 2023 FFWI award trophy which was held at the H Usmar Ismail Film Center (PPHUI) Kuningan, South Jakarta, Friday (27/10/2023) afternoon.

"Yes, I was crying, because to be honest, as long as I have been acting, this is the first award I have received," said TJ Ruth after winning the award.

He also cried because the filming process for the film "Why Do You Love Me" was still underway during the Covid-19 pandemic.

"The filming was right during PPKM. Wow, it was a real struggle, it was about to be finished, but it had to be postponed. So I had to maintain my weight, isn't Endang in the film fat," said the 43 year old woman.

Apart from that, the woman whose real name was Ruth Purnamasari cried was because she remembered her dog who died, when she was at the gala premiere of the film "Why Do You Love Me".

05. MNC Pictures Joins APFI, Expects National Film Industry Quality to Increase

JAKARTA - The Association of Indonesian Film Companies (APFI) is ready to cooperate with all stakeholders to advance the Indonesian film industry. Therefore, APFI welcomes MNC Pictures and Temata Studio as the newest members.

So the number of APFI members is 12 production houses. Previously joined members were Starvision, Max Pictures, Falcon Pictures, Rapi Films, Soraya Intercine Films, Mizan Pictures, Mahaka Pictures, Hitmaker, Screenplay Bumilangit, and Visinema.

The chairman of APFI, Chand Parwez, is also happy about the joining of MNC Pictures and Temata Studio. He believes this step will strengthen the Indonesian film industry, which is starting to revive in the midst of a pandemic.

09. Rano Karno Wins the Best Male Lead Award

JAKARTA - Rano Karno has proven that he still has qualities and abilities that are not inferior to younger actors, even though he has taken a long break from acting. At the 2019 Indonesian Movie Actors (IMA) Awards, Thursday (14/3/2019) the actor was chosen as the Best Main Male Actor.

After the 2019 Indonesian Movie Actors (IMA) Awards' Favorite Main Male Actor was won by Gading Marten through the film Love For Sale, the Best Main Male Actor was won by veteran actor Rano Karno, Thursday (14/3/2019). This award was chosen directly by the jury consisting of different backgrounds.

Even though he has been absent from the film industry for so long, Rano's acting skills in every film are unquestionable. To win this prestigious award at the 2019 IMA Awards is not easy. The reason is, in this category, Rano has to compete with other talented actors who are much younger than him. They are Adipati Dolken, Ario Bayu, Augie Fantinus, Dion Wiyoko, Gading Marten and Ringgo Agus Rahman.

In his remarks when receiving this award, Rano said Si Doel The Movie 2 would be released on Eid al-Fitr 2019.

10. The film Mariposa is on the Google search list for 2020, here's Angga Yunanda's reaction

jpnn.com, JAKARTA - Actor Angga Yunanda said he was surprised to learn that the film Mariposa was included in Google's search list for 2020. He did not expect that the film he starred in with Zara, ex-JKT48, would receive such high enthusiasm. "It's really amazing to see the Mariposa film on Google's trending list this year along with other films," said Angga Yunanda, Saturday (12/12).

"At first, I never expected with the enthusiasm of all my friends to become one of the trending ones on Google," he continued. According to Angga, there are many factors that have made the film Mariposa one of Google's trending ranks throughout 2020. "What is certain is that I am very grateful and can't wait to see this film re-run in theaters on December 31," he concluded.

It is known, throughout 2020, there were five Indonesian films most searched for based on the Year in Search 2020 list released by Google.com. The five films compete between popular Korean film and drama titles such as Crash Landing on You, It's Okay to Not Be Okay, to the film Mulan. Interestingly, Mariposa, which will return on December 31, 2020, is one of the most searched films on Google. (jlo/jpnn)
