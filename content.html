A collection of films that have been produced by IDZ Film

01. The Big Four

The Big 4 tells the story of Dina (Putri Marino), a detective who is known to be straight and adheres to his principles as a police officer. One day, Dina finds that her father (Budi Ros) died mysteriously. Dina tries to uncover the mystery of her father's death by following the clues she finds. These various clues then led Dina to a tropical island called Bersi Island. There, Dina actually got information about another side of her father that she had never known about. The father turned out to be the leader of a group of assassins who had many students.

02. Gadis Kretek

The film Gadis Kretek is an Indonesian drama film released in 2022. This film was directed by Ifa Isfansyah and produced by Visinema Pictures. This film stars Dian Sastrowardoyo, Nicholas Saputra, and Putri Marino. This film tells the story of Kartini's life journey, a woman who was born and raised in a family of kretek cigarette producers. Kartini has to struggle to realize her dream of becoming a doctor, amidst traditions and customs that restrict women.

03. 13 Bom di Jakarta

The film Ketika Berhenti Di Sini the story of a woman named Ika (Marsha Timothy) who is struggling to deal with the death of her husband. Ika has to take care of her two small children, as well as facing various problems in her life. Ika then meets a man named Arya (Chicco Jerikho) who is also grieving the loss of his wife. Arya and Ika comfort and strengthen each other.

04. Pernikahan Dini

The film Pernikahan Dini tells the story of a teenage girl named Ayu (Natasha Wilona) who is forced to marry an adult man named Raka (Bryan Domani). Ayu married because her family forced her to. Raka himself also doesn't want this marriage. However, they both have to learn to live a married life they don't want.

05. Layangan Putus

The film Kite Putus is an Indonesian drama film released in 2021. This film was directed by Benni Setiawan and produced by MD Pictures. This film stars Reza Rahadian, Putri Marino, and Anya Geraldine. This film tells the story of Kinan, a woman who has just given birth to her first child. Kinan is happy with her marriage to Aris, a successful businessman. However, Kinan's happiness began to falter when he found evidence that Aris was having an affair with Lydia, a woman who worked in his office. Kinan also decides to divorce Aris.

06. Gundala

The film Gundala is an Indonesian superhero film released in 2019. This film was directed by Joko Anwar and produced by Bumilangit Cinematic Universe. This film stars Abimana Aryasatya, Tara Basro, and Bront Palarae. This film tells the story of Sancaka, a boy who has electrical powers. Sancaka must fight to fight evil and protect the weak.

07. Tenggelamnya Kapal Van Der Wijck

The film Tenggelamnya Kapal Van Der Wijck is an Indonesian drama film released in 2013. This film was directed by Hanung Bramantyo and produced by Multivision Plus. This film stars Herjunot Ali, Pevita Pearce, and Reza Rahadian. This film tells the story of Zainuddin, a poor young man who falls in love with Hayati, a noble girl. Zainuddin and Hayati have to face various obstacles to be together, including differences in social class and the arranged marriage that Hayati's family has prepared.

08. Budi Pekerti

The film Budi Pekerti is an Indonesian drama film released in 2019. This film was directed by Garin Nugroho and produced by Rapi Films. This film stars Reza Rahadian, Putri Marino, and Ayu Laksmi. This film tells the story of Budi, a man who works as a teacher at an elementary school in a remote village. Budi has to face various challenges to educate children in the village, including poverty, ignorance and violence.

09. Sewu Dino

Karina (Karina Suwandi) is a mysterious woman who lives in a remote village in Central Java. He has very strong black magic powers, and he uses his powers to take revenge on the people who have hurt him. One day, a group of young people consisting of Nadia (Mikha Tambayong), Azka (Gisellma Firmansyah), and Rehan (Ari Irham) went to the village to create viral content for their social media. They didn't know that they were entering Karina's territory.

10. Siksa Neraka

Four siblings, Saleh (Rizky Fachrel), Fajar (Kiesha Alvaro), Tyas (Ratu Sofya), and Azizah (Nayla Purnama), have heard stories about heaven and hell since childhood. They were educated strictly by their father (Ariyo Wahab), a young ustad who was well respected in the village. One night, on their way to the opposite village without their parents knowing, Saleh and his younger siblings were swept away by a strong river current and disappeared. Saleh then woke up in another realm, the hell that his father always talked about.
